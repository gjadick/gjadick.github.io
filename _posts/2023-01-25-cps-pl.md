title: X-ray phase contrast + Chicago Public Schools
---

Today, UofC Medical Physics successfully coordinated a professional learning day for Chicago Public School (CPS) teachers, covering waves and their applications in medical physics. As our Diversity & Outreach Committee student co-director, I helped organize the event and also gave a research talk on CT imaging with x-ray phase contrast. The day was the result of months of collaborative work with the UofC Cancer Center and CPS. We're looking forward to next year!
