Our work on emphysema quantifications was recently published in *Chest*, answering the research question: how do CT scan imaging parameters affect the accuracy of emphysema-based quantifications and biomarkers? See the article [here](https://journal.chestnet.org/article/S0012-3692(22)04203-9/fulltext).

E Abadi, **G Jadick**, DA Lynch, WP Segars, E Samei. ”Emphysema
quantifications with CT: Assessing the effects of acquisition protocols and imaging parameters
using virtual imaging trials.” 2022 *Chest*.
