My first refereed paper as a first author has been published in *Physics in Medicine & Biology*, showing our work developing a scanner-specific framework for simulating CT images with tube current modulation. See the article [here](https://iopscience.iop.org/article/10.1088/1361-6560/ac2269/meta).
