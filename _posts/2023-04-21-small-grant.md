title: Outreach Small Grant Award 
---

My small grant proposal has been selected for an award of $1,000 through the UChicago Diversity Research and Small Grants Program. These funds will allow our medical physics outreach team to create a comprehensive set of hands-on teaching activities to utilize in outreach demonstrations for local Chicago Public School students. Thank you to the UChicago Office of Diversity & Inclusion for supporting the outreach mission of our graduate program in medical physics.
