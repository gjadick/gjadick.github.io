I've been selected to receive a National Science Foundation (NSF) Graduate Research Fellowship! I'm honored to join the 2023 NSF GRFP cohort along with my brilliant classmate Chisondi Warioba.

The fellowship will fund the next three years of my PhD research. I'm excited to continue my work in medical imaging, developing tools to help us see beyond what is possible with the human eye alone (even with conventional imaging technology).

[Chisondi Warioba and Gia Jadick receive the NSF Graduate Research Fellowship](https://medphys.uchicago.edu/news-archive/chisondi-warioba-and-gia-jadick-receive-nsf-graduate-research-fellowship)
