I've been selected to receive a National Science Foundation (NSF) Graduate Research Fellowship. My planned research will focus on quantitative phase-contrast x-ray computed tomography for soft tissue imaging.  This fellowship will fund the next three years of my PhD research. I'm excited to continue my work in medical imaging.
