I won second place in the Young Investigator Symposium at this year's AAPM Midwest Chapter Meeting for my talk "Dual energy CT imaging with a megavoltage spectrum." The meeting was a great opportunity to share research and learn from the excellent work being done by our medical physics colleagues near the Chicago area. I'm looking forward to next year.
