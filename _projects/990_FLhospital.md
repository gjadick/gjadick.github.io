My first research experience was as a clinical research intern with the Digestive Health Institute at Florida Hospital Tampa (now AdventHealth), where I worked with Sharona Ross and Alexander Rosemurgy. I primarily focused on [achalasia](https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/abs/10.1177/000313481708300931), an esophageal disorder, and [patient outcomes after surgical intervention](https://linkinghub.elsevier.com/retrieve/pii/S0002961016306924). My statistical analysis and writing resulted in two co-authored publications.
