Bacterial membrane response to hyperosmotic shock
============

As a 4th-year undergraduate at Duke, I worked with Christoph Schmidt and Renata Garces to characterize the bacterial membrane response of *E. Coli* to hyperosmotic shock. I observed different degrees of plasmolysis with increasing osmotic pressure and wrote an image analysis script in Python to automate the calculation of elastic bending energy for the membranes. When the pandemic shut down all wet lab activities in early 2020, I shifted my focus to image analysis and learned to run fluid membrane simulations with Surface Evolver.
