My current project in the La Rivière lab is on quantifying best-achievable image quality for a dual energy CT system that jointly utilizes megavoltage (MV) and kilovoltage (kV) spectra. Such a system could be useful for improving image contrast in radiation therapy settings. I have developed a single-ray toy model and a fan-beam CT raytracing simulation for this purpose. I recently presented this work at [SPIE Medical Imaging 2023](https://medphys.uchicago.edu/news-archive/2023-spie-medical-imaging-symposium).
